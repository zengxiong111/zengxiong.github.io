My research studies how to design robot learning systems that are both data-driven and rigorous.

## Current Focus

- **Koopman learning**: statistical limits for learning useful observables.
- **Direct data-driven control**: noise sensitivity and robust design for LQR-style synthesis.
- **System identification**: minimax rates under bounded noise.
- **Learning to stabilize**: fundamental hardness and structural benefits.
