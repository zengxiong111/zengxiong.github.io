I am a Ph.D. student in the ECE Department at the University of Michigan (started Fall 2021), advised by [Prof. Necmiye Ozay](https://web.eecs.umich.edu/~necmiye/).

## Research

I work on rigorous robot learning system design, with an emphasis on:

- System identification under realistic noise models.
- Learning-based control with stability and robustness guarantees.
- Statistical limits of Koopman observable learning.

Core theme: **Real2Sim2Real = System Identification + Learning-based Control**.

For a detailed overview, see [my PhD proposal slides](https://github.com/zengxiong111/zengxiong.github.io/releases/download/v1.0/phd_propsoal_Xiong_Zeng_part.pdf).
